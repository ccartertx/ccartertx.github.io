Blog Post: An Introduction

January 13, 2014

This will be my second blog in less than a year. I started a WordPress blog in May 2013 to start documenting efforts in homebrewing and cooking (and whatever else came along, like photography).

I joined MakerSquare to challenge myself and learn new skills that I could apply at work, developing online courses, but also to help me grow this side project of brewing and blogging.

This blog will detail my journey at MakerSquare and also be a place to test out new knowledge and talk about the process.
